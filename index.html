1984
Starbucks introduced the Caffè Latte, revolutionizing how coffee was consumed in the United States. This drink became the cornerstone of our menu and helped define the modern coffeehouse experience.
1996
Starbucks partnered with PepsiCo to create the bottled Frappuccino, bringing its popular café beverage to grocery stores. This marked Starbucks' first venture into the ready-to-drink beverage market.
2008
Introduction of Pike Place Roast: A smooth, everyday coffee blend named after their first store at Pike Place Market.
2020
Expansion into Plant-Based Beverages: Added oat milk and plant-based options like the Oatmilk Honey Latte and Beyond Meat products.
